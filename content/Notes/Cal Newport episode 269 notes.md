- Parent contact emails
- Filter out Damron’s emails and Costello’s

## Don’t get started
- Circle back to a thing with more information to see if it’s worth pursuing
- Research the people who are doing what you want to do
- The Middle Way between not taking action and taking action blindly
- Really see if what you’re pursuing fits
## Treat your mind like a private garden
- Limit what you consume as content to only what aligns with your values
- Pay attention to what matters and leave the rest
- What you pay attention to is your reality
- Gratitude is a mindset
## Think like an artist, work like an accountant
- You can be more creative when you’re organized, despite popular belief
- Focus on organizational productivity, think more creative thoughts and have big ideas
## Office hours for parents
- Set up a time of the week where you make yourself available to parent calls
- Figure out how to make it easy for parents to call the desk phone
