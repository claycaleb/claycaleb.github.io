What doesn’t work:
- Pressure
- Rationalizing
- Punishment

What works:
1. Slow things down
2. Keep the calm
3. Listen to understand
4. Use connection and understanding to build a solution

“the paradox of power”—namely, the harder we push the more likely we are to be met with resistance.

“Put simply, reasoning doesn’t work because people aren’t primarily reason-driven. And pressure doesn’t work because feeling like your back is up against a wall only increases emotional arousal.“
