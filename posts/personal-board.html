Personal Board of Directors

In Shane Parrish's book Clear Thinking, he writes that the people we spend our time with are the biggest influence on our behavior. He says that we learn best from others. If you don't have access to your role models you can imagine who you would choose as your personal board of directors. Your board can include real, living people, people from history, people you know personally, and even fictional characters.
