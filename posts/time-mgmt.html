Time Blocking and Cool Calendars

I like planning how to spend my time. It feels productive and like I have control over time (it’s nice to pretend). I use Cal Newport’s time-blocking method to schedule my alone/work time. It’s nearly impossible to time-block a day spent with two kids, so I’ve given up trying to structure days when my kids are home with me. Learn more about time-blocking here.

I also enjoy using this Year at a Glance calendar I copied from Ali Abdaal’s time management method. I made a similar school-related calendar as well. You’ll have to replace the dates based on your district calendar. Feel free to copy these and use them!
